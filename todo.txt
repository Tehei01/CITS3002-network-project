    - 3. Hosts probably don't need a routing table entry for their own subnet and a default-ish entry for the other.
        Real hosts usually have: "my subnet → direct" and "everything else → default gateway." Your current setup works, 
        but consider whether you want to generalise this to a default route ("0.0.0.0/0") for anything not in the local subnet. 
        For a two-subnet assignment it doesn't matter; just flagging it.
